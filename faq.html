Shelf Share - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shelf Share?

Shelf Share is a platform for book enthusiasts to swap books with others in their community, promoting sustainability and shared knowledge.

How does the swapping process work?

You can list books you’d like to share and browse books from others. Once you find a match, arrange a swap through the platform!

Is there a fee to join Shelf Share?

No, joining Shelf Share is completely free. Some users may choose to negotiate shipping or delivery costs for swaps.

Can I swap books internationally?

Yes, international swaps are possible. Be sure to consider shipping times and costs when coordinating with other users.

What if I don’t find a book I’m looking for?

You can set up alerts for specific titles, and Shelf Share will notify you when someone lists them.
