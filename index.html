About Us

ShelfShare is a community-driven platform that connects local book lovers, allowing them to easily exchange books with one another. By bringing together individuals who live near each other, ShelfShare fosters a shared love of reading while promoting sustainability through book reuse. Subscribers can browse a virtual bookshelf of available titles, arrange swaps with nearby members, and enjoy a constantly changing selection of books without the need to purchase new ones. It's an affordable and eco-friendly way to explore new stories and connect with fellow readers in your area.   Read more

Start Sharing

Joining ShelfShare is easy and quick! Simply visit our website and create an account. Once you're registered, enter your location to connect with nearby book lovers. ShelfShare offers flexible subscription tiers to fit your reading habits: the basic plan lets you swap one book at a time, while premium and family plans provide access to more swaps and additional features like priority access to high-demand books. After selecting your plan, you can browse local listings, offer your own books, and start exchanging stories with fellow readers near you!   Read more
